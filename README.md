# 📿 Ruqyah Shariyah (الرقية الشرعية) – Islamic Healing Guide

A comprehensive, step-by-step guide to performing authentic Ruqyah (spiritual healing) according to the Quran and Sunnah.

---

## 🌟 What is Ruqyah?

**Ruqyah** (رُقية) is the Islamic practice of reciting Quranic verses and authentic supplications to seek healing from spiritual and physical ailments. It is a Sunnah taught by Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and Angel Jibril (AS).

&gt; *"And We send down of the Quran that which is healing and mercy for the believers."*  
&gt; — **Quran 17:82**

## 🛠️ How to Use This Guide

### For Self-Ruqyah:
1. **Prepare**: Make wudu, face Qibla, sit comfortably
2. **Recite**: Follow Steps 1–4 daily (minimum)
3. **Blow**: After each step, blow into your hands and wipe over your body
4. **Water**: For Steps 5–8, recite into water and drink/bathe
5. **Consistency**: Perform daily for 7, 40, or 90 days depending on severity

### For Treating Others:
- Have the patient sit facing Qibla
- Place your right hand on their forehead or affected area
- Recite clearly and loudly
- Observe for reactions (yawning, burping, crying, etc.)

## ⚠️ Important Conditions

✅ **Valid Ruqyah Requirements:**
- Belief that only Allah heals
- Clear, comprehensible Arabic
- Quran & authentic Sunnah only
- No shirk or haram elements

❌ **Invalidates Ruqyah:**
- Wearing amulets/taweez
- Visiting fortune-tellers
- Mixing with innovations (bid'ah)
- Charging money for ruqyah
